**Cyberwarfare** = use of technology to penetrate and attack another nation's computer systems and networks in an effort to cause damage or disrupt services.

<u>The purpose of Cyberwarfare:</U>
- Main reason for resorting to cyberwarfare is to gain advantage over adversaries, whether they are nations or competitors.
	- Gather compromised information and/or defense secrets
		- compromised sensitive data can give attackers leverage to blackmail
		- stealing secrets can narrow gaps in its military capabilities
	- Impact another nation's infrastructure and to damage it
		- disruption, chaos, roads/power damage, exchange of goods/services halted, and impact everyday citizens.

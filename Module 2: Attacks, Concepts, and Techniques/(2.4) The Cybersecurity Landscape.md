# (2.4) The Cybersecurity Landscape 

**Cryptocurrency** = digital money that can be used to buy goods and services, using strong encryption techniques to secure online transactions.
- CC (Cryptocurrency) owners can keep their money in encrypted, digital wallets & transactions between 2 wallets are recorded in decentralized/ledger/blockchain system.
- every ~10 minutes, special computers collect data on latest CC transactions and turning them into mathematical puzzles to maintain confidentially → these are verified through a process called "mining" (army of miners working on PCs to solve puzzles and authenticate transactions).
- Once verified, ledger is updated and copied and disseminated worldwide.

**Cryptojacking** = threat hiding on user computer, phone, server, etc. using that machine's resources to mine CC without user consent.
